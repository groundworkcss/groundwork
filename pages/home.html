Example Layouts to Get You Going

These are really just to introduce you how things can be done and (hopefully) spark your imagination.

NOTE: The very first thing you should do when using Groundwork is change the color code in the `$button-color` variable in `src/scss/_settings`.

If I see ugly teal buttons and nav menus on thousands of websites in a year I will be the saddest panda. If you're terrible at design or don't know how to pick nice colors then just go to Colour Lovers and pick from the gazillion beautiful palettes people have put together for you.
